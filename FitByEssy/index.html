    About Me


    I'm a Certified Personal Trainer who has
    dedicated my life to changing lives by sharing my creative workouts,
    life experiences, and words of wisdom.

    I have inspired a couple of young women in Kenya. I saw an opportunity to improve my health at my teenage years and excel beyond the norm of “women shouldn’t be in fitness”. Despite the lack of support and resources available to me, I invested my time learning about the human body & how the mind influences how we shape our thoughts & habits


    MISSION

    To empower women around the world to feel confident, healthy and strong from the inside-out through the practice of fitness and mindfulness.
